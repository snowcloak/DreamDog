Here at DreamDog, we know it may be difficult to decide on what dog you should make yours. That's why we created an application that makes this process a lot easier for you!

DreamDog allows all dog lovers to enter some information about themselves that generates an accurate selection of dog breed that would be best suitable for you!

DreamDog also enables you to connect amongst one another via 'Bark Messenger'! There's tons of fun stuff to explore on DreamDog -- including an interactive game!
